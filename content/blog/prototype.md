Our website prototype consists of three pages: A homepage, a Hell Page, and a Heaven Page. Right now our prototype homepage contains a basic layout linking to our other pages. But we plan to make it more animated and interactive to better communicate the purpose of the site. The goal of the website is to create a free-range creative space where users can express themselves through art and poetry. The Heaven and Hell page have similar layouts and functionality. Both include panels on the side for interactive stickers. We implemented a drag-and-drop featue using the fabric.js library for people to place images directly onto the canvas. Inside the canvas, we plan to have an arrangement of words for people to drag-and-drop into the canvas to poetically communicate a message.
