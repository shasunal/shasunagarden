Frog & Toad

Readings 2

  • 27 February 2026

The chapter starts off with explaining how the Digital realm is now a part of our daily lives and it is changing what we call ‘home’. I definitely feel that it's not possible to completely escape from technology today and we are becoming very dependent on it. Most people wouldn’t blink an eye to accepting cookies or to checking the box under ‘terms of service’. Our data is constantly being gathered and shared now more than ever. I think about how social media apps are tailored to my interests. It is shaping our ‘home’ and who we are. The chapter talks about surveillance capitalism - how our data is taken and analyzed to better market products towards us. I think about how the ads on Instagram have exactly what I am looking for (I’ve spent money on instagram ads) and my feed has what I want to see. I almost don’t think twice about it because of how it reflects my interests and ‘knows’ me. Looking back, social media has definitely had an influence on my opinions and interests to some extent. The article about Facebook talks about how advertisers target users with racial proxies or categories that correlate with a certain race. Again, this makes me think about the online bubble we are in with only topics that interest us, or targeted at us. We miss out on other news and opinions.
