Project 2

  • 22 February 2026

For our second project my group decided to make a baby yoda tarot reading website but with random movie quotes. We will be uploading a star wars tarot design we found on the internet and using html/css to stimulate the card-picking process from a deck of cards. We are using a random movie quote API to replace actual tarot card readings. There will be a baby yoda illustration explaining the tarot cards and guiding you along the website. At the end of your tarot reading, the page will be translated to baby yoda language using our second API. Our project will have a total of 3 pages linked chronologically to one another and the player will navigate with clicking and hovering.


I will be handling the structure and layout of the website with html/css. I will be communicating with the back end for which kind of action to perform and interactions that are happening. I will be paying attention to the overall theme we are following, the font, alignments, hierarchy, and any interactive elements. I will also be working with asset illustration to make sure images are displayed correctly.
